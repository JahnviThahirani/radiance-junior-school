Frequently Asked Questions

Everything parents ask before joining the Radiance family — answered honestly.

We welcome children from 2 years of age for our Playgroup program. Our programs run from 2–6 years across Playgroup, Nursery, LKG and UKG. The exact age cutoff is assessed at the time of admission based on the child's readiness and our current capacity.
Our curriculum is built on Seven Developmental Pillars — addressing not just academic readiness, but cognitive, emotional, social, physical and creative growth simultaneously. Every activity is purposefully designed, not just time-filling. We follow a play-based learning approach where children discover concepts through exploration, not rote instruction.
We have flexible half-day and full-day program timings to suit working parents. Our batches are intentionally kept small with a low student-to-teacher ratio to ensure every child receives personalised attention, care and support throughout their day. Please reach out to us for the specific schedule for each program.
We share detailed, colourful Progress Reports with parents each term — covering all seven developmental areas holistically. Beyond formal reports, our teachers maintain regular communication through parent-teacher meetings, activity updates and our dedicated WhatsApp channel where you can see your child's daily learning moments.
